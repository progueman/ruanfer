6 Sins and a Forgiveness

by Nad

Oh, my God I am heartily sorry
for having offended you...

On the first day of the week
I learned how to tease
Making little kids cry....

On the second day of the week
I learned how to be disrespectful
Shouting to my elders...

On the 3rd day of the week
I learned how to lie
Lying to my parents...

On the 4th day of the week
I learned how to cheat
Cheating in all my tests...

On the 5th day of the week
I learned how to steal
Stealing from my good friends...

On the 6th day of the week
I learned how to kill
Killing all those I hate...

On the 7th day of the week
I realized all my faults
I thought you wouldn’t forgive me
But you did!
