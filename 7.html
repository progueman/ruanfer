Dahil sa sobrang bait mo, you decided to pass the right stairs. Buti na lang! Nakita mo kasi, na ang mga dumadaan sa wrong staircase ay pinapagalitan ni M.A.T.H.I.C (Mechanically automated technology and HomEconomics instructing Computer.) Mathic is the one of several of La Salle's automatic and fully armored, combat cyborgs.

Nakarating ka sa classroom and you notice that everyone is quiet! The reason they were quiet was that they were studying. You wanted to ask them, pero baka pagkamalan kang nerdo tulad ni Paolo. If you didn't ask them, baka bumagsak ka sa isang unexpected quiz.

What do you do?
